Features & Capabilities

AI–Driven Symptom Analysis

Chatbot uses Claude Opus 4.5 to analyze symptoms, classify urgency, and suggest the right medical speciality.

Real-Time Appointment Scheduling

Calendly provides available slots and instant appointment creation.

Booking, Reschedule & Cancel

Patients can book, reschedule, or cancel appointments anytime via the chatbot.

Unified Appointment Management

All bookings can be viewed or managed via a single Calendly link in the chatbot.

Complete Flow (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: Open Chatbot

Chatbot collects patient details and guides through booking.

Step 2: Symptom Input

Claude Opus 4.5 analyzes symptoms and suggests the right speciality.

Step 3: Show Slots

Bot fetches real-time slot availability from Calendly.

Step 4: Book Appointment

Slot confirmed → Calendly booking created → confirmation shown in chatbot.

Step 5: Reschedule & Cancel

Patients can manage bookings anytime via the unified Calendly link.
