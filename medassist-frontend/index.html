Features & Capabilities

Full Chatbot Booking Flow

Patient provides name, phone, email → OTP verification → age → chooses speciality or shares symptoms → AI recommends doctor or books general appointment.

Reschedule & Cancel (Chatbot)

Patient selects Reschedule/Cancel from dropdown → bot asks phone/booking ID → updates Google Sheet & sends confirmation SMS/email/chat response.

Receptionist Google Sheet Sync

Every booking (new/reschedule/cancel) is written to a centralized Google Sheet. Receptionist uses dropdown column (PRESENT/ABSENT/CANCELLED/RESCHEDULED) to track visits.

Operator Widget (Zoho SalesIQ)

Operator widget shows patient info (name, phone, email, age, slot, doctor, clinic, map link). Operators can chat, reschedule, or cancel from the widget — actions immediately update the Sheet.

Automated SMS & Email Confirmations

After booking, patient receives SMS & email with clinic name, map link, reception number, doctor, slot time, and booking ID. Also available in the chat window.

Reminders & No-Show Handling

Reminders sent 1 hour before appointment. If patient doesn't check-in and receptionist marks PRESENT not clicked within 20 minutes, bot sends a "No-show" follow-up automatically.

Attendance Flow (Receptionist)

Receptionist verifies booking at counter using booking ID / phone → marks PRESENT in Sheet (bot confirms). If ABSENT, bot sends a polite missed-appointment message.

Symptom → Doctor Mapping (AI)

Simple keyword-based AI routing (extensible to ML) — maps symptoms to specialist suggestions; fallback to general physician if unclear.

Multilingual & Voice Support

Chatbot supports English + native language via SalesIQ widget and can accept voice input (browser mic) to create bookings faster.

Complete Booking Summary Everywhere

All booking details (name, phone, age, email, doctor, slot type/time, clinic info) are visible in chat, SMS, email and operator widget for seamless communication.

Complete Flow (step-by-step)

1

User visits website & opens chatbot

Chatbot greets user and collects name, phone, email and runs OTP verification.

2

Collect age & symptoms / speciality

Bot asks age, desired speciality or symptoms. AI suggests a doctor if symptoms are clear.

3

Book appointment

Bot shows slots → user confirms → booking saved to Google Sheet & operator widget. SMS/email/Chat confirmations sent with clinic/map/contact.

4

Reminders & Arrival

Reminder sent 1 hour before. On arrival, receptionist verifies via booking ID/phone and marks PRESENT in Sheet.

5

No-show & Follow-up

If PRESENT not marked within 20 mins, bot sends a 'didn't come' follow-up. If receptionist marks ABSENT, bot sends a missed-appointment message.

6

Operator Chat & Management

Operator widget displays full patient context; operators can reschedule/cancel or chat — actions update the Sheet and notify patient.
